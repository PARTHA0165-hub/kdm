About Us

Afford Medical Technologies Pvt. Ltd. (CIN: U72200TG2007PTC056067) traces its origin to a partnership firm, Technomed formed in 1998 by a group of dedicated Biomedical Engineers. Affordmed prides itself on decades of experience in providing quality medical device support across various medical specialities including Critical Care, Cardio-Vascular Surgery, Orthopaedics, Laparoscopy, Diagnostics, etc. True to its name, the company constantly strives to provide and sustain comprehensive solutions to all our customers across medical institutions, hospitals & labs with a focus on Affordability, Technology, and Innovation. While being headquartered at Hyderabad, we have a pan-India customer base and staff ensuring timely response during critical medical procedures.

CEO & Managing Director

Mr. Sankarambadi Nadathur Dayanidhi (DIN: 01651057), an electronics and communications engineer by training, is the Founder-CEO and Managing Director of Afford Medical Technologies Private Limited. In a career spanning over 30 years, he has vast hands-on experience and knowledge across critical cardiology and cardiovascular surgery equipment. Beginning his entrepreneurial journey in 1998, he's been instrumental in democratising access to affordable medical devices for numerous hospitals and medical institutions in the country. He also spearheaded the indigenous research, development, and manufacturing of diagnostics lab equipment since 2010 through Dalko Diagnostics Private Limited. In the last decade, he's also been instrumental in expanding the service and product offering to other developing and under- developed economics including Nigeria, Fiji Islands, Myanmar to bring about affordable access to medical devices in these countries. He is presently spearheading the next phase of product innovation and development of the company. Prior to beginning his entrepreneurial journey, he served in key leadership roles at Trivitron Medical Systems Private Limited and Instruments & Machines Inc.
